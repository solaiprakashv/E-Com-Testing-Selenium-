## Tips for Using These Bullets

1. **Customize**: Adjust numbers and percentages based on your actual results
2. **Be Specific**: Use concrete metrics (18 test cases, 100% pass rate)
3. **Action Verbs**: Start with strong verbs (Developed, Implemented, Designed)
4. **Results-Focused**: Highlight outcomes and impact
5. **Technical Keywords**: Include tools and technologies for ATS systems
6. **Quantify**: Use numbers to show scale and impact
